## The challenge

Bloom Café was losing reservation requests every weekend — voicemails went unreturned, and their old website couldn't accept bookings. Walk-in dependency made revenue unpredictable.

## What we built

- A Next.js website with server-rendered menus and online reservations
- An AI receptionist trained on opening hours, dietary policies, and seating rules
- Two-way Google Calendar sync so the kitchen sees bookings in real time

## Results in 60 days

- **3x online reservations** with no increase in marketing spend
- **15+ hours/week saved** previously spent on reservation calls
- **1.4-second average page load** on mobile (down from 4.8s)

## Stack

`Next.js · React Server Components · OpenAI · Google Calendar API · Sanity CMS`
