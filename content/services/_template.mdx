## Overview

Replace your busiest hour with an AI agent that never sleeps. Our AI receptionist is custom-trained on your services, hours, FAQs, and preferred tone, then connected to your calendar and CRM so every conversation ends in an action — not a missed message.

## Who it's for

- Clinics, salons, and law firms losing leads after hours
- Real estate agents juggling property inquiries and showings
- Restaurants and cafés handling reservation calls during peak service
- Trades and service businesses without dedicated front-desk staff

## What's included

- Conversational agent (voice + chat) trained on your knowledge base
- Calendar integration (Google, Outlook, Calendly)
- CRM sync (HubSpot, Pipedrive, Salesforce, Zoho)
- Multilingual support (English, French, Arabic, more on request)
- Monthly conversation analytics & tuning

## How we deliver

1. **Discovery** — 30-min call to map intents and edge cases.
2. **Training** — we ingest your FAQs, product data, and tone guide.
3. **Integration** — calendar, CRM, and notification channels wired up.
4. **Soft launch** — supervised conversations for the first 48 hours.
5. **Handoff** — dashboard access, monthly tuning, ongoing support.

## Frequently asked

> Will it sound robotic?
> No. We tune voice, pacing, and vocabulary to match your brand.

> What happens if it doesn't know an answer?
> Graceful fallback to a human handoff with full conversation context.
